The Problem

Car accidents are a crucial issue currently present in our world today. Road crashes are the leading cause of death among people aged 5-29.

Key Factors

A couple significant factors that affect where crashes occur include the sizes of roads as well as the weather.

Prepare for Future

It is important to be able to track which conditions make a road dangerous in order to help city planners adjust in the future.

Community

It's important that people also know which roads are the safest so that they can prepare ahead of time before driving.
