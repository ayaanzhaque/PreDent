Our Mission

The Problem

Car accidents are a crucial issue currently present in our world today. Road crashes are the leading cause of death among people aged 5-29. This can be largely attributed to poorly designed roads and infrastructure.

Key Factors

A couple significant factors that affect where crashes occur include the sizes of roads. billboards and signs, and the weather. We aim to analyze these factors and fit a model to these attributes.

City Officials

It is important to be able to track which conditions make a road dangerous and where potential hotspots for crashes are in order to help city planners adjust in the future.

The Community

People should also be able to know which roads and paths are the safest so that they can prepare ahead of time before driving. This ultimately promotes smarter travel.

Our Process

Locating Hotspots

Using ML, we are able to detect crash hotspots by taking in features such as the size of the road, stop signs, and billboards. We also look at constantly changing features such as weather and concentration of cars.

Application

Driver Use

Governments also are able to use this to upload their city data in order to help them discover collision hotspots and figure out how to adjust for the future.

Web Design

Government Use

Users can view where in their city the hotspots are, allowing them to adjust before driving, They can also help governments collect data by reporting crashes which gets processed in our database.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Don't people know if someone is suicidal?

It might be possible to tell that someone is upset or even depressed, but it is incredibly difficult to determine if someone has suicidal tendancies as a person. It is possible to tell based on their messages and their phrases, which means using our algorithm is the best way to distinctively tell if someone is suicidal or not.

Who is this site specifically meant for?

This site is mainly meant for friends of those who are going through depression so they can accurately see specifically what their friend is dealing with. Additionally, therapists and doctors can also use our algorithm to better understand their patients especially when their phrases are hard to distinguish and cryptic.

How does the COVID-19 relate to our website?

COVID-19 has exacerbated an existing problem, and we donâ€™t believe more people should be dying from suicides due to COVID-19 stress than COVID itself. From our algorithm to our pairing software, our application is built towards specifically dealing with suicidal tendencies due to COVID. We address specific reasons for accumulating stress during quarantine, use data specific top online communication, and more to make SuiSense as effective as possible during COVID-19.

Why is our site better than existing models?

Unlike current existing models, which send an automated robot to help interact with the depressed person, ours is a comprehensive step by step process that will involve an experienced properly trained therapist and will eventually root back to the friend. This is important because the person that is depressed is much more likely to be more receptive to advice from their friends and real human beings rather than automated messages.
